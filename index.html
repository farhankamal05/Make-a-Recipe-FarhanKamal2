Italian Croissant Sandwich with chips

Ah yes a mix of Itallian and french this mix taste great and can easily be made.

CREDIT: ALEX FARNUM; STYLING: KAREN SHINTO

Ingredients

  • 1 Costco croissant
  • 1/4 slice of Butter
  • 1/2 cup of Marinara Sauce
  • 1/2 cup of Pepper Jack Cheese
  • 1 Bag of Chips (Your Choice)

Directions

  1. Slice your Croissant in half with a knife
  2. Add 1/4 slice of butter on the pan and wait for it to melt
  3. Once melted Place both Sliced Croissant's on the pan and make sure to cook both sides until it becomes flaky or hard (or you can do it for 1 minute)
  4. Once cooked add 1/2 cup of Marinara Sauce on both slice Croissant
  5. Then add 1/2 cup of Pepper Jack cheese on one of the sliced Croissant's and spread it
  6. OPTIONAL add some toppings or meats on top of the sliced Croissant
  7. Place the other sliced croissant on top and put it in the microwave for 2-3 minutes
  8. Once microwaved go ahead and plate it by adding the bag chips then the Croissant Sandwich
  9. Now enjoy!
